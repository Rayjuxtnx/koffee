About Me

For over seven years, I have been preserving memories through the lens of my camera, capturing the beauty in fleeting moments. My journey began with my grandfather's old film camera, and since then, I've dedicated myself to the art of photography with a vintage soul.

Specializing in wedding, portrait, and nature photography, I approach each project with a storyteller's eye, seeking to capture the emotion, connection, and authenticity of every moment. My style is characterized by natural lighting, soft tones, and a timeless quality that harkens back to the golden age of film photography.
